# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind and respectful to other people
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

> Note: the "punishment" mechanics in this project are a playful productivity
> metaphor aimed at an AI coding assistant. They are not a model for how we
> treat **people**. Be good to each other.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the
maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and
fairly.
